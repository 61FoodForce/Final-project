    • Food Waste

    On a day to day basis, most people wouldn't think twice about the left over food they are planing on throwing away. In the United States, More than one third of available food gets thrown away. The food that we throw away without second thought end up in landfills. There, all the food decomposes and creates large amounts of a gas called methane. like Carbon Dioxide, Methane is considered a green house gas due to how easily it can absorbs the sun and heat up the local environment causing global warming. The big differance between Methane and CO2, is Methane is 28X more powerful, meaning 28X more harmful to the environment. The effects of letting methane run wild wont just effect our environment but us as people as well. causing smog and effecting people with asma

    Hunger in America

    In America alone, there are millions of people that suffer through hunger, unsure where they will get their next meal. From a young age, If we don't have a safe and consistent source of food can cause cognitive defects, chronic despises later in life, and other long term health issues. If not having enough money to buy food wasn't enough trouble already, most of these families also need pay other bills fees. Life for people in though situations is already a battle, but we can help bring them peace of mind knowing that they can save money and eat meals.
